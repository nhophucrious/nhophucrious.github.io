# My site is now powered by Hugo
It's always good to get some historical context. I built the original version with a simple stack: plain HTML, CSS, and JS. Back then, and up to very recently, it had been an extension of my résumé, you know, all that experience, skills, contact info... were given colours and links and what not.

I was happy with it, until there was a need to add more content. Because of the simple nature of the legacy site, simply adding anything was a big hassle. I'm not a web developer either, which adds more complexity. Therefore, Hugo comes up as a natural progression, an evolution, to what I had.

# What's next?
I'll be updating the content of my blogs and my projects more frequently. I realised that I may have wasted a bit of time, and I wanted to put that into good use.

Expect more technical talk, tool reviews, and my learning progression.

I don't know if anyone would read them. I would, in the future, and that's always what I wanted to do with my now (kinda deceased) Wordpress page. I usually go through my old paper exams in high school and university, and they always fascinate me - the cheapest alternative to time traveling. So, I hope this site would turn out the same!
